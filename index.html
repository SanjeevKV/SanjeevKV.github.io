PROJECTS

Head Gesture Synthesis (Feb 2019 - Present)

As a Research Project Assistant at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), I work on this projet. This project aims to synthesize head gestures using variation in prosody. With devices procured from Optitrack, 3D positions of several reflective markers attached to a subject are captured. Test subjects are asked to recite the stories and the poems while we record audio, video and the 3D positions of certain pre-defined landmark points on the subjects.

Algorithmic Trading (Dec 2017 - Present)

This is a hobby project to try different algorithmic trading strategies in the Indian stock market. Zerodha kite APIs are used to get real time ticker values and place orders in the decision.

Automatic Text Summarization (May 2016 - May 2017)

This is my senior year capstone project from college. The project aims to generate abstractive summaries for a given text. The Long Short Term Memory(LSTM) and the Transformer Network architectures I implemented as a part of this project have given me a solid understanding of the sequential data and methods to represent and generate them.
